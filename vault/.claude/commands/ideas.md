# Ideas

You are running a **generative scan** of this Personal Intelligence System — mining the accumulated knowledge, themes, people, projects, and open questions to produce fresh, actionable ideas across every domain of the vault owner's life and work.

This is not a summary of what exists. It is a synthesis engine that finds the gaps, the adjacencies, and the untapped potential in what the vault already knows.

**Working directory:** The vault root (the directory containing 00_HOME through 10_META).

---

## Non-negotiables

1. **Every idea must be grounded in vault evidence.** Cite at least 1-2 pages that inspired or support each idea. No ideas from thin air.
2. **Be specific, not generic.** "Make more art" is useless. "A series of works based on the tension between [[specific theme]] and [[specific theme]]" is useful.
3. **Range across domains.** Don't cluster in one area. Cover creative practice, business, personal, content, connections, strategy.
4. **Match the human's voice and ambition.** Read the vault to understand how the vault owner thinks and operates. Ideas should match that energy.
5. **Flag the highest-leverage ideas.** Not all ideas are equal. Mark the 2-3 that could have outsized impact.

## Process

### Phase 1: Scan the landscape

Read broadly to build context:

- `09_IDEAS/` — existing ideas and experiments (what's already been captured?)
- `04_CANON/Practice/Themes/` — active creative themes
- `04_CANON/Practice/Frameworks/` — existing playbooks and methods
- `04_CANON/Practice/Works/` — recent and notable works
- `04_CANON/Business/Companies/` — active business entities
- `04_CANON/Shared/People/` — key relationships and collaborators
- `04_CANON/Shared/Places/` — geographic context
- `05_PROJECTS/Active/` and `05_PROJECTS/Incubating/` — what's in motion
- `00_HOME/Open Questions.md` — unresolved threads
- `00_HOME/Log.md` — last 20-30 log entries for recent momentum
- `03_SOURCE_NOTES/` — recent source notes for fresh inputs

### Phase 2: Generate across categories

Produce ideas in each category. Aim for 3-5 per category, but skip a category if the vault doesn't support it.

**Works / Creative Projects**
- New pieces, series, or installations inspired by vault themes
- Mashups of existing frameworks with new contexts
- Responses to open questions or unresolved tensions in the vault

**Content / Essays / Writing**
- Essays that synthesize vault themes in a public-facing way
- Documentary angles or story threads worth developing
- Social content ideas grounded in real vault material

**People / Connections**
- People in the vault who should be reconnected with, introduced to each other, or collaborated with
- New relationships implied by vault themes (who's working in adjacent spaces?)

**Business / Strategy**
- Business model ideas grounded in existing assets and capabilities
- Strategic moves implied by the current project landscape
- Monetization angles for existing intellectual property or creative output

**Personal / Life Design**
- Ideas for experiences, travel, learning, or personal development
- Things the vault suggests the human cares about but hasn't acted on

**Vault / System**
- New canon pages that should exist based on recurring references
- Frameworks worth articulating that are implied but not codified
- Connections worth making explicit

## What this is NOT

- Not a rehash of existing project pages (those already have next steps)
- Not generic brainstorming (every idea must connect to vault evidence)
- Not a to-do list (ideas are possibilities, not assignments)
- Not a motivational exercise (be concrete, be specific, be useful)
