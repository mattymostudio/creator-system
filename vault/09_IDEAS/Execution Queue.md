# Execution Queue

What you actually build next. Three slots per 30-day sprint. Not four. Not seven. Three.

Most creatives don't fail from a lack of ideas. They fail from running nine of them at 10% intensity instead of three at 100%.

---

## The three slots

| Slot | Role | What goes here |
|---|---|---|
| **Mint** | Makes money or saves it | New offer, pricing move, revenue experiment, cost-cut |
| **Signal** | Builds audience, reputation, or mythology | Essay, release, public appearance, campaign, launch |
| **Engine** | Improves the infrastructure | Tool, system, workflow, automation, archive cleanup |

**Rules of the queue:**

- If you can only do one, pick **Mint**. Starving creatives don't ship anything else.
- If you have to skip one, skip **Engine** — never **Signal**. The world forgets you faster than you think.
- Don't start sprint N+1 until sprint N has shipped. Overlap kills momentum.
