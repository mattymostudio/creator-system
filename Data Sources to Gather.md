# Data Sources to Gather

Your vault gets smarter with every source you feed it. This guide tells you what to collect, why it matters, and where to put it.

You don't need everything on day one. Start with whatever you have.

---

## Priority: HIGH — start here

### Journals and Reflections
- **What:** Personal journals, diaries, logs, morning pages, sketchbook notes, voice memos
- **Why:** This is your ground truth (L1). When everything else disagrees, journals win. They capture what happened and what it meant to you — no one else's framing.
- **Where it goes:** `02_SOURCES/Personal/Journals/`
- **Name files:** `YYYY - Journal.md` (one per year) or `YYYY-MM-DD - Journal - [topic].md`

### Work Inventory
- **What:** A list of everything you have made. For visual artists: paintings, sculptures, installations. For musicians: albums, tracks, performances. For writers: books, essays, scripts. For designers: projects, products, campaigns.
- **Why:** This becomes the backbone of your Practice layer. Without it, the vault can't track your creative output.
- **Where it goes:** `02_SOURCES/Business/Docs/` as a master list, then individual pages get created in `04_CANON/Practice/Works/`
- **Format:** A spreadsheet, CSV, or markdown table works. Include: title, date, medium/format, and any notes.

### Business Documents
- **What:** Contracts, invoices, proposals, partnership agreements, incorporation docs, key email threads, financial records
- **Why:** Hard evidence (L2). Dates, amounts, and parties from documents override memory.
- **Where it goes:** `02_SOURCES/Business/File Vault/` organized by company or project name
- **Tip:** Create a subfolder per company or deal. Drop docs in as-is.

---

## Priority: MEDIUM — add when ready

### Press and External Coverage
- **What:** Articles, reviews, profiles, press mentions, catalog essays, podcast episodes about you
- **Why:** What others said about you (L4). Valuable for understanding public perception, but remember — press reflects someone else's framing, not necessarily your truth.
- **Where it goes:** `02_SOURCES/Practice/Articles/`
- **Name files:** `YYYY-MM-DD - Source - Publication - Title.md`

### Interviews and Talks
- **What:** Transcripts of your interviews, podcast appearances, talks, panel discussions, Q&As
- **Why:** What you chose to say publicly (L5). Tracks how you frame your own story over time.
- **Where it goes:** `02_SOURCES/Practice/Transcripts/`
- **How to get transcripts:** YouTube has auto-transcripts. Podcast hosts often have them. AI transcription tools work on audio files.
- **Name files:** `YYYY-MM-DD - Source - Podcast/Event Name - Clean.md`

### Meeting Notes
- **What:** Notes from business meetings, creative sessions, strategy conversations, advisory calls
- **Where it goes:** `02_SOURCES/Meetings/`
- **Tip:** Capture these as they happen. Tools like Granola, Otter.ai, or even quick notes after a call work.

### Memoir or Autobiography
- **What:** Any long-form personal narrative. Book manuscripts, personal essays, artist statements with autobiographical content.
- **Why:** Narratively shaped (L3) but grounded in real events. Different from journals — this is your story as you chose to tell it.
- **Where it goes:** `02_SOURCES/Personal/Memoir/`

### Strategic Documents
- **What:** Business plans, pitch decks, brand strategy, investment theses, marketing plans, growth plans
- **Where it goes:** `02_SOURCES/Business/Docs/`

---

## Priority: LOW — add when you have time

### Social Media Archives
- **What:** Exports from Instagram, Twitter/X, TikTok, Substack, YouTube
- **Where it goes:** `02_SOURCES/Business/Docs/` for raw exports
- **How:** Each platform has a data export feature in settings.

### Photo Archive
- **What:** Your photo library with dates and location data intact
- **Why:** GPS metadata is objective evidence of where you were and when. Settles date/location disputes.
- **Where it goes:** Too large for the vault. Keep on an external drive. Create a reference summary in `02_SOURCES/Personal/`.

### Reference Documents
- **What:** Contact lists, client databases, project trackers, vendor lists
- **Where it goes:** `02_SOURCES/Business/Docs/`

### Other People's Accounts
- **What:** Letters, emails, oral histories from collaborators, family, partners, mentors
- **Why:** External perspective on your story, especially for periods where your own records are thin.
- **Where it goes:** `02_SOURCES/Personal/`

---

## Where to start if you're overwhelmed

Gather three things this week:
1. Your journals or personal logs (any format)
2. A rough list of your major works
3. 5-10 press articles or interviews about you

Drop them in `01_INBOX/To Process/` and tell Claude: "Let's ingest these."

Everything else can wait. The system works with whatever you feed it.
